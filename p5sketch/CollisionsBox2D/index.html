Expo Growth Simulator

A quick and dirty simulator of disease spread that reproduces the resulting logistic map of population growth.

We can observe an early exponential growth followed by the ceiling effect/

One can try different scenarii:

  • Normal case (click on Reset to start over): every one can move freely
  • Social distancing: 3/4 of the population is immobilised
  • Partial lockedown: 1/4 of the population is locked and can (almost) not meet the other... But complete lockedown is impossible, so there is a small gap.
